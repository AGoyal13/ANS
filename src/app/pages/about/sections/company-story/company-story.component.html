1966

Founded by a Visionary

ANS Constructions Pvt Ltd was founded by Late Shri Amar Nath Sharma Ji (05 Dec 1931 – 07 Dec 2007) — a self-made man who rose from humble roots in Himachal Pradesh to build one of northern India's most respected construction firms. Starting with irrigation works in Uttar Pradesh and Punjab, his vision was simple: deliver quality, build trust.

1990s

Scaling Into Industry

Through the 1990s, ANS expanded into fertilizer plant construction, oil refinery civil works, and institutional buildings. Long-term relationships were forged with industry giants including Chambal Fertilizers (since 1990), Tata Chemicals (since 1990), and MRPL, Mangalore (since 1993) — relationships that continue to this day.

2000s

National Footprint

The new millennium brought landmark projects — civil works for Petronet LNG at Dahej, the HPCA International Cricket Stadium at Dharamshala, the BITS Pilani Goa Campus, and mega irrigation contracts in Madhya Pradesh. ANS was now operating concurrently across 10+ states.

Today

A Mega-Project Conglomerate

Under the leadership of Sh. Mehinder Sharma (Managing Director), ANS Constructions Pvt. Ltd. today commands a turnover of over ₹147 Crore (FY 2024), with active projects for DMRC, BARC, NHPC, IOCL, Adani, and Talcher Fertilizers. ISO 9001 and OHSAS 18001 certified, the company remains committed to its founding creed: We Remain At Your Service.
